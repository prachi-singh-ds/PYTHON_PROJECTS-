# ✈️ Airline Ticket Pricing Pattern Analysis  
*A Data Science Project on Price Behaviour, Seasonal Patterns & Travel Insights*

---

## 📌 **Project Overview**

This project performs an in-depth **Airline Ticket Pricing Analysis** to identify:
- Price variation across airlines  
- Impact of travel class, flight duration, and number of stops  
- Booking window effect (early booking vs late booking price surge)  
- Seasonal price trends (high-demand travel months)  

The aim is to provide **data-backed insights** useful for:
✔ Airline pricing teams  
✔ Travel agencies  
✔ Passengers planning budget travel  
✔ Data science learners  

---


## 🧠 **Key Objectives**
1. Understand how airline ticket prices behave across various features.  
2. Discover strong predictors of high ticket prices.  
3. Build insights that support real-world business decisions.  
4. Practice EDA and data visualization on a structured dataset.  

---


## 🗂️ **Dataset Summary**
- **Rows:** 100  
- **Columns:** 10  
- **Source:** Manually curated dataset simulating real airline pricing patterns  
- **Features Include:**  
  - Airline  
  - Source & Destination  
  - Class (Economy / Business)  
  - Duration (hrs)  
  - Booking days before travel  
  - Stops (Non-stop / 1-stop / 2-stop)  
  - Price  
  - Month (extracted from date)

## 📈 **Key Insights (Executive Summary)**

### ✨ **Business-Level Findings**
- **Vistara** has the highest average ticket price.  
- **Indigo** offers the lowest prices consistently.  
- **Business Class** shows maximum price variance.  
- **Early bookings significantly reduce ticket prices.**  
- **December & April** are peak expensive months (seasonal surge).  
- **Non-stop flights** are more expensive than stopover flights.  
- **Flight duration** strongly influences ticket cost.
