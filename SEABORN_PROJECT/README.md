
⚡ EV Charging Station Analytics
Advanced Data Visualization using Python, Pandas & Seaborn


📌 Project Overview

The rapid adoption of Electric Vehicles (EVs) has created a growing demand for reliable and efficient EV charging infrastructure. Understanding how charging stations perform in terms of utilization, pricing, revenue generation, and customer experience is essential for improving EV ecosystems.

This project performs an Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on EV Charging Station data to uncover meaningful insights about charging behavior and station performance.

Using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn, this project analyzes operational metrics such as:

Charging station utilization

Revenue generation

Energy delivery efficiency

Customer ratings

Charging duration patterns

The goal is to transform raw operational data into visual insights that help understand EV charging station performance.



🎯 Key Objectives

• Analyze charging price distribution across stations

• Compare revenue generated by different charger types

• Identify cities with highest charging utilization

• Understand relationship between charging price and customer ratings

• Analyze charging duration patterns

• Study monthly revenue trends

📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains operational information related to EV charging stations.

Key Dataset Features

• City
• Charger Type
• Charging Sessions
• Average Charging Time
• Energy Delivered (kWh)
• Price per kWh
• Revenue Generated
• Customer Rating
• Utilization Rate

This dataset enables analysis of charging demand patterns, revenue performance, and station efficiency.

📈 Business Insights

• Fast chargers tend to generate higher revenue due to faster charging cycles and higher throughput.

• Cities with higher utilization rates indicate stronger EV adoption and charging demand.

• Stations delivering more energy per session contribute significantly to revenue generation.

• Charging duration varies significantly depending on charger type and station demand.

• Higher customer ratings often correlate with better service quality and station efficiency.

• Revenue trends reveal periods of increased EV charging demand, which can help plan infrastructure expansion.
