📘 Global Fuel Price Dynamics — Visualization Project

Data Science | Energy Analytics | Matplotlib | Pandas | EDA


📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes global fuel pricing patterns using advanced visualization and exploratory data analysis.
Dataset contains 100+ realistic daily records across:

- 20 Countries
- 3 Fuel Types – Petrol, Diesel, CNG
- Crude Oil Prices
- Inflation Rates
- Currency Exchange Rates
- Monthly & Yearly Trends
- This analysis highlights:
- Economic factors influencing global fuel prices
- Country-wise pricing variations
- Seasonal & volatility patterns
- Fuel-type behavior across regions



🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze global fuel price movement
- Compare countries based on fuel price
- Compare Petrol vs Diesel vs CNG
- Study volatility & instability in fuel markets
- Understand inflation & crude oil impact
- Identify monthly seasonal patterns
- Build corporate-grade visualizations



🧠 Key Insights Delivered

- Countries with highest & lowest average fuel prices
- Crude oil’s direct influence on fuel price
- Strong inflation → fuel price correlation
- CNG is consistently the cheapest fuel type
- Seasonality visible in monthly median
- Volatility curves show unstable pricing periods
- Rolling averages produce cleaner trend lines
